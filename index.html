What We Do

Rise Together Initiative(RTI) is a registered charitable organization under CBO, headquartered in Isingiro District, Nakivaale Settlement. Founded and led by refugees, we provide disaster relief, education, and social support to vulnerable populations.

Disabilities

We advocate for equal opportunities and the inclusion of persons with disabilities by promoting their rights, dignity, and active participation in all areas of community life. We work to create an inclusive society where individuals with disabilities have equal access to education, employment, healthcare, social services, and community activities without discrimination or barriers.

Through awareness campaigns, community engagement, and support programs, we encourage acceptance, accessibility, and empowerment for persons with disabilities. We also provide support that helps individuals develop skills, build confidence, and participate fully in social, economic, and cultural activities within their communities.

Undergoing early pregnancy for women

We provide care, guidance, and emotional support for women undergoing early pregnancy. Our organization is committed to helping women feel safe, informed, and supported during this important stage of life.

Through counseling, health education, and community support programs, we empower women to make informed decisions about their health and future.

Agriculture development

Our organization promotes agriculture development to improve food security, reduce poverty, and strengthen livelihoods within communities. We support farmers, women, and youth by providing knowledge, training, and resources that help increase agricultural productivity and sustainability.

Education & Skills Training

We provide educational programs, workshops, and practical skills training designed to equip individuals with the knowledge and abilities needed to succeed in everyday life and contribute positively to society.

Our training programs focus on personal development, leadership, economic empowerment, and peacebuilding.

Peacebuilding & Conflict Prevention

We organize workshops and training sessions in peacebuilding, conflict prevention, and conflict resolution to help communities develop peaceful ways of solving disagreements and preventing violence.

Through these programs, participants learn the importance of dialogue, understanding, cooperation, and mutual respect. We also promote human rights awareness and encourage active participation in creating safe and inclusive communities.

Positive Peace & Peace Education

Our peace education initiatives focus on promoting positive peace — not only the absence of conflict, but also the presence of justice, equality, opportunity, and social harmony.

We teach individuals and communities how to build healthy relationships, strengthen social connections, and contribute to long-term peace and stability.

Entrepreneurship Training

We support individuals by providing entrepreneurship training that helps them develop business ideas, start small enterprises, and become financially independent. Participants learn practical business skills such as planning, problem-solving, innovation, and business management to help them create sustainable livelihoods.

Financial Literacy

Our financial literacy programs teach essential money management skills, including budgeting, saving, financial planning, and responsible spending. These trainings are designed to help individuals and families improve their financial stability and make informed economic decisions for a better future.

Leadership Skills Development

We believe strong leadership is essential for community growth and transformation. Our leadership development programs help participants build confidence, communication skills, teamwork abilities, and decision-making skills.

We encourage young people and community members to become responsible leaders who inspire positive change within their communities. Through all our programs, we aim to empower people with the tools, knowledge, and confidence needed to build peaceful, resilient, and thriving communities.
